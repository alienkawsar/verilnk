# VeriLnk Invoice Wording Specification

Status: Finance-approved baseline  
Language version: `v1.0`  
Applies to: PDF invoices generated by backend invoice renderer

Any invoice text changes must be reviewed against this document.

## Approved Phrases

### Payment Terms
- `PAYMENT_TERMS_NET_15`: "Payment terms: Net 15."
- `PAYMENT_TERMS_DUE_ON_RECEIPT`: "Payment terms: Due upon receipt."
- `PAYMENT_TERMS_NET_15_UNLESS_STATED`: "Payment terms: Net 15 (unless otherwise stated in your agreement)."

### Subscription / SaaS Notice
- `SAAS_AUTO_RENEWAL_NOTICE`: "Payments may be processed automatically for subscription renewals."

### Late Payment
- `LATE_PAYMENT_NOTICE`: "For past-due invoices, please contact billing to confirm remittance details."

### Tax / VAT
- `TAX_INCLUDED`: "Applicable taxes are included as shown."
- `TAX_EXCLUDED`: "Taxes, if applicable, are not included unless stated otherwise."
- `TAX_CROSS_BORDER`: "Taxes may be subject to local regulations and are the responsibility of the recipient, where applicable."

### Contact and Validity
- `BILLING_CONTACT`: "For billing inquiries, contact support@verilnk.com."
- `SYSTEM_GENERATED_VALIDITY`: "This invoice is system-generated and valid without signature."

### Legal Traceability
- Version line format: `Invoice language version: v1.0`

## Selection Rules

### Billing Type Rules
- SaaS / self-serve invoices:
  - Due upon receipt
  - Auto-renewal notice
  - Tax/VAT line (single applicable line)
  - Late-payment line (if applicable)
  - Billing contact
  - System-generated validity line
- Enterprise / contract invoices:
  - Net 15
  - Tax/VAT line (single applicable line)
  - Late-payment line (if applicable)
  - Billing contact
  - System-generated validity line
- Unknown billing type:
  - Net 15 (unless otherwise stated in your agreement)
  - Tax/VAT line (single applicable line)
  - Late-payment line (if applicable)
  - Billing contact
  - System-generated validity line

### Late Payment Rule
Show `LATE_PAYMENT_NOTICE` only when:
- invoice is past due (`dueAt < now`), and
- status is not `PAID`, `VOID`, or `REFUNDED`.

### Tax / VAT Rule (single line only)
Priority order:
1. If cross-border context applies, use `TAX_CROSS_BORDER`.
2. Else if tax amount is greater than zero, use `TAX_INCLUDED`.
3. Else use `TAX_EXCLUDED`.

## Compliance Notes
- Wording is neutral and non-promotional.
- No threats, penalties, or legal escalation language in late-payment copy.
- Footer remains text-only for print and archive stability.
- Language version line must remain visible for audit traceability.
